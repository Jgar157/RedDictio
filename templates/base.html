How this works

Our program collects comments from the most popular posts from some of the most popular subreddits. The program sends analyzes those comments, utilizing a neural network, to detect the likelihood of a comment containing hateful speech. The neural network DOES NOT tell indicate whether or not the comment definitively contains hate.
